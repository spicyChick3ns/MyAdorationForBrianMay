BRIAN MAY

Brian May was born in 1947. He is a musician, songwriter, astrophysicist, and animal rights advocate.

He is the vocalist and lead guitarist for the rock band Queen. Prior to Queen, he was in the band Smile. Aside from the guitar, May also plays the piano, banjolele, and ukulele.

Brian May holds a BSc for physics and math, as well as a PhD in astrophysics from Imperial College.

"The guitar has a kind of grit and excitement possessed by nothing else."
